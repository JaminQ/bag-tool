usage: bag-tool <command>

You can use these <command>:
  -v        View bag-tool version
  help      Get docs
  init      Init your src-dir and config-file
  clean     Clear dest-dir
  build     Compile your src-dir to dest-dir
  start     Build once, Open your browser and load,
            then Watch your src-dir to live-reload

你可以输入`bag-tool help -c`来获取中文文档。
